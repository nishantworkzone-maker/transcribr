FAQ

Common questions

What audio formats do you support? +
We support MP3, MP4, M4A, WAV, OGG, OPUS, WEBM, and more. If you're unsure, just try uploading — we'll let you know if the format isn't supported.
How accurate is the transcription? +
For clean audio in English, accuracy is typically 95–98%. For accented speech or noisy recordings, we recommend the Balanced or Most Accurate mode.
Is my audio stored or shared? +
No. Audio files are processed and immediately discarded. We never store your recordings. Transcripts are saved to your account only if you're logged in, and you can delete them anytime.
Can I try it without signing up? +
Yes. You get 3 free transcriptions as a guest — no account needed. Download or copy your transcript before leaving as guests sessions don't save.
What's the difference between Fast, Balanced, and Most Accurate? +
Fast uses Groq's Whisper model — results in under 30 seconds. Balanced uses Deepgram Nova-2, better for calls with multiple speakers. Most Accurate uses AssemblyAI for noisy audio and complex vocabulary.
